About Legacy

About

Legacy School is a Learner-Centered Community that encourages all its members to immerse themselves in all aspects of school life as they explore their journey of self-discovery.

As a community, we value and encourage a culture of collaborative learning, positive and respectful relationships within and outside our community. Our commitment is to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment where students and teachers are engaged through authentic learning opportunities that inspire them to develop creativity, confidence, and resilience to become independent and innovative thinkers, creative problem solvers and stimulated learners who will excel as empathetic, open-minded and principled learners. Our education extends far beyond the classroom. Learning at Legacy School takes place at various platforms and students are offered a range of opportunities to excel in academic, creative, social, cultural, sporting and responsible citizenship endeavours. Students are empowered to explore and pursue their interests and strengths to the best of their ability. Students are encouraged to question, to make mistakes and challenge the status quo. We encourage our Staff and Students to achieve their personal best, be sensitive to the rights and needs of others and ethical in their practices. High standards and expectations for each are the integral values of our School. Our endeavours are supported by caring, compassionate, committed and experienced faculty and staff, who work as partners in learning with students. They seek to deepen knowledge, provide enriching learning experiences, while gently attempting to stretch intellectual boundaries and explore new challenges. The focus is to effectively support engagement in the world of the future, honing crucial skills, values and dispositions. Of course, this website cannot replace a visit to our School; we warmly welcome you to visit us to discover what makes our community and education at Legacy School so special.

Learning at Legacy

  • Approach to Learning

    Society today, needs learned and cultured citizens who have a purpose and an intention to make a positive difference in their lives and the world around them. At Legacy School, we ignite the mind to think, explore and find a distinctive meaning to life. We maintain a 1:8 Teacher to Student ratio to provide individual attention and facilitate meaningful interaction in all our learning sessions. All our educators are qualified, experienced, trained and enjoy engaging with children in the process of learning. We adopt established international teaching standards and practices to enable relevant learning through inquiry and application based approaches; we incorporate technology and a range of co-scholastic activities that foster the skills of open communication, analysis, creative thinking, problem-solving and collaboration in each of our learners.

  • Co-Scholastics

    Legacy School offers a wide array of co-scholastic activities for every child to discover and explore their talent and interests. Art, craft, dance, music, theatre, sports, yoga, student clubs, educational field trips, guest speakers, outbound learning programs, student conferences, Model United Nations, University visits and various workshops are integrated to complement a child’s learning. We also encourage regular participation in various cultural, literary, sports, arts and business events and competitions at the intra and inter-school levels. Legacy School has partnered with Edusports – India’s first and leading sports enterprise working with schools to provide a unique sporting experience. The scientifically designed and structured Edusports program for children helps physical education and sports get to the same level of rigor, focus, and involvement that exists in academic subjects in schools. Sports Offered: Football, Athletics, Basketball, Cricket, Swimming, Tennis, and Badminton. We also offer an array of sports for students to pursue after school hours on weekdays and football on weekends with two professional football academies.

Admissions

Right to Education

Right to Education

Applicants seeking admission in 2018-19 will be directly impacted by the implementation of the Right to Education Act (RTE) when it comes to the availability of seats at two points of entry (in Lower Kindergarten and Grade 1) and with regards to the school fees. Therefore, if you are not already familiar with the RTE Act, we would strongly recommend that you take some time to familiarize yourself with and review the articles online (refer to leading newspapers’ websites) or refer to the The Gazette of India with regards to the Right to Education Act that has been passed as an act of Parliament and which is being implemented by the various Education Departments of each State across India.

Admission Policies

Admission Policies

We are happy to receive inquiries from parents and encourage them to visit the school with or without their children to experience our school culture. We have Open Days calendar on our website and welcome you throughout the year. We share all about our school through an interactive presentation and a campus tour. In case families are coming from other cities, states or overseas are advised to schedule an appointment to visit our campus at a mutually convenient time and meet our educators. Once you are convinced that we are the right fit for you, we share application forms with you, review your application and make an offer to you. We have limited openings in some grades and encourage you to apply in time Our admission offers are based on merit, and each potential applicant will receive fair treatment regardless of gender, caste, religion, ethnicity, nationality or examination board.
